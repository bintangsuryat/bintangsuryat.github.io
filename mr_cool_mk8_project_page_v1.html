Fire Fighting Hexapod Robot 2019 V1

- Develop fire fighting robot system for mission to find & extinguish a flame on one of the rooms in the labyrinth
- This Robot Project for Indonesia Robotics Contest in Regional 3 Level

Mechanics & manufacture:

• 6 legged (hexapod) configuration system with 3 degree of freedom (3 servos per leg) • Acrylic 3 mm and 5 mm material for body frame • Acrylic Body sheild for body protect while following on the wall maze • Co2 gas inflator mechanism

Hardware:

• Atmega 2560 as main microcontroller • Atmega 328 as slave censor microcontroller • Atmega 32 as slave invers kinematics algorithm microcontroller • Servo Controller SSC-32 for control leg servos • 6 Ping Ultrasonic Censor for Navigation, mapping, & wall (maze) following • Compass Censor • Sound Activation censor • Multiplex phototransistor for flame censor • IR Gp2d12 censor for avoiding dog obstacle & avoiding furniture • 3 TPA 81 flame Censor • 18 servos to legs configuration • 1 servo to open Co2 gas inflator mechanism

Program:

• Inverse kinematics algorithm for dynamic walk gait • Maze mapping (path planning) algorithm to finding a flame room • Avoiding furniture and dog obstacle algorithm • Flame detection algorithm • Back Tracking algorithm to back to home after extinguish a flame
