Fire Fighting Hexapod Robot 2017

- Develop fire fighting robot system for mission to find & extinguish a flame on one of the rooms in the labyrinth
- This Robot Project for Indonesia Robotics Contest 2017 in Regional 3 & National Level

Mechanics & manufacture:

• 6 legged (hexapod) system with 3 degree of freedom (3 servos per leg) • Acrylic 3 mm and 5 mm material for body frame

Hardware:

• Atmega 128 as main microcontroller • Atmega 8 as slave censsor microcontroller • Atmega 32 as slave invers kinematics algorithm microcontroller • Servo Controller SSC-32 for control leg servos • Ultrasonic Ping Censor for Navigation & wall (maze) following • Multiplex phototransistor for flame censor • TPA 81 fame Censor

Program:

• Inverse kinematics algorithm for dynamic walk gait • Wall following using timer algorithm for finding a flame room • Avoiding furniture and obstacle algorithm • Flame detection algorithm
